About

I obtained my B.A. and M.A. in Political Science from the University of the Philippines Diliman and the Central European University respectively. My main research interests include quantitative research methods, data science applied to voting behavior, and patronage politics.

Teaching

I am currently teaching three undergraduate courses at University of the Philippines Los Baños:

  • POSC 10: Principles of Politics and Governance
  • POSC 141: Contemporary Political Ideologies
  • SOSC 2: Social, Political and Economic Thought
